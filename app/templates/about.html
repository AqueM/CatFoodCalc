This tool is a side-project to hone my coding and testing knowledge, as well as my research skills.

I love cats, and in cat-people circles the topic of food quality as well as amount you should feed is always a hot one.
Because of this, I decided it would be neat to have a tool that could calculate your cat's needs based on scientific knowledge.

This concept evolved over time to include a couple more features and ended up as this little app. Hope you enjoy it.

Sources

  • FEDIAF Nutrition Guide
  • NRC Guidelines (2006)
  • AAFCO Methods For Substantiating Nutritional Adequacy Of Dog And Cat Foods (2014)
